# Space Scenes in the Godot Engine 

Feel free to use as a desktop or whatever you like! 

## The power of Godot 

Talk about how Godot is capable of making far more detailed and beautiful 3D scenes than people tend to think.

People who think that you can't make "good graphics" in Godot are very wrong, as I am lacking in humility enough to suggest I've shown in some of my demos. 

I am unsure if I'll do anything more in this specific area, but it's been a lot of fun!
